Software

The Choose of software is a big part in being a designer, but I would say it doesn't really matter that much, It all comes to what you prefer, workflow wise and how the software feels and look like. so it can be anything that you prefer. Mostly people go for the adobe pack since that is a industry standard and is very well known. now more recently alot of diffrent software has come up to people radar but most people stick to the adobe pack.

Recommended Design Software

Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Photoshop is arguably the most popular graphic design software and is used by millions of artists around the world.
Sketch.
Sketch is a Mac-only graphic design program that focuses mainly on digital design.
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Illustrator is a design software that is centered around vector design.
Lunacy
Lunacy is a free, super-fast graphic software for UI, UX, and web design.
